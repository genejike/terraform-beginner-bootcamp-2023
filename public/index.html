Jollof Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and set it aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Add the diced red and green bell peppers and continue to sauté for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Add the washed rice and stir to coat it with the tomato mixture.
  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until it's fully cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot. Enjoy your Jollof Rice!
